About

I am a physician trained at Government Medical College and Hospital (GMCH), Chandigarh, where I ranked 3rd in a class of 150 and held a four-year merit scholarship. I passed USMLE Step 1 in November 2024 and am a certified BLS/ACLS provider (AHA). My research centres on clinical pharmacology and drug safety — including PCSK9-inhibitor and statin safety signals, pharmacovigilance using the FDA Adverse Event Reporting System, and the role of artificial intelligence in clinical pharmacology — with peer-reviewed publications and a first-author oral presentation at the British Pharmacological Society's Pharmacology 2024 meeting.
